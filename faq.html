Add Homescreen Widgets (1x1, 2x1, 4x1) for Android

Make sure Final Countdown is installed on the device internal storage and not on the SD card. Touch the All Apps icon on your Home screen. Touch Widgets tab at the top of the screen. From the main Widgets screen, you can Move between screens to find Final Countdown: Swipe left or right. Drag a countdown widget (1x1, 2x1, 4x1) to a Home screen: Touch and hold the widget's icon, slide your finger where you want to place it, and lift your finger. Choose an event to activate widget.

You can share your live countdowns including the background image with anyone

Simply share the countdown via txt, email, Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, WhatsApp or any other application. The person clicks the link you shared and they will have the same countdown. http://fncd.net/v?id=Q8_laTR

Countdown freezes or is jumpy

Task Killer/Task Manager apps can cause countdown widgets to freeze. If you have any of them installed, configure them to ignore Final Countdown.

Dates or times are off or seem inaccurate

The only thing that can cause dates to jump or seems is if the timezone is changed. Make sure that the two "same" events have the same timezone settings. By default, your phone will set the default time zone for an event.
